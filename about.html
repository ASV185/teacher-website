About me

Welcome to my page!

My name is Alan Ventura, and I am a teacher in El Paso, Texas. I am originally from Ciudad Juarez, Mexico, and due to awonderful life event, I started a new life in the United States. As I began my career as an educator, I realized how this career could be difficult and daunting, especially for newly formed teachers. For this reason, I chose to build a website with the purpose of sharing my presentations, bell ringers, and even assignments for free! Yes, free! I am not monetizing my content because I understand that not all teachers have the financial capacity to purchase class materials. I also hope veteran teachers can benefit from this website. If you want to share your great ideas, assignments, presentations, or bell ringers, I would be happy to share them in this website and give you credit. On the other hand, If you have any suggestions that can improve the quality of my content, please let me know. Feel free to use the content. And Remember, teachers are a beacon of light in a sea of vast darkness. Sapere Aude!
